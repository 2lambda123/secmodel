This Securitisation model models cash flows and Internal Rate of Returns for an Asset Portfolio, Senior Bond (90% of Asset Portfolio) and Subordinated Loan (10% of Asset Portfolio). The Maturity is set at 30 years and Repayments are pro-rate and monthly.

This model includes a 1% fee calculated on the Asset Portfolio balance and a Reserve Fund (1% of the original Asset Portfolio).

Recovery of Defaulted assets is set at 50% and is recognised in the same month. User can set the Amount of the Asset Portfolio, fixed coupons of the Assets, Senior Bond and Suborindated Loan and Prepayment and Default amounts.

Future Steps - enable Floating Rates and Default and Prepayment Curves.
